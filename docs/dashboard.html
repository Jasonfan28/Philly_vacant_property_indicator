About This Dashboard

Philly Stat 360 · Residential Vacancy Risk Model

What this shows

An interactive map of predicted vacancy risk scores for residential parcels across Philadelphia. The ward choropleth shows average risk by ward; zoom to level 13+ for individual parcel risk colors.
